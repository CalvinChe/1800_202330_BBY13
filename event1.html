VanDusen is a 55 acre oasis in the heart of Vancouver and the fall colours are spectacular.

November 23rd is the day before the official opening of the Festival of Lights. Cost is still $8.90 to get in whereas if you go during the Festival of Lights it can cost up to $25. Each member will pay for their own entrance fee. If you have a membership, you do not have to pay to go into the garden.

Hopefully, the lights will be turned on as the lights need to be tested before the official opening.

We will meet in the Garden Cafe (Truffles) next to the admissions desk for coffee/ snack/ light lunch before heading into the garden.
If you cannot attend after signing up, I ask that you cancel by 12pm November 22nd.
